## Introduction

#### Welcome to Envless?

Envless is the end-to-end encrypted, open source and frictionless way to share and manage app secrets across teams, platforms, infrastructures, CI/CD. It works with any programming language, frameworks, hosting platforms and infrastructures, CI/CD pipelines. It fits right into developer workflows with branches and pull-requests.
